Canal Street Market is a carefully curated retail market, food hall & community space open year-round at 265 Canal Street.

A New Kind of Market

Merging retail, food, art, and culture, Canal Street Market highlights top retail and design concepts,
restaurants, and up-and-coming players in the downtown New York City community.
